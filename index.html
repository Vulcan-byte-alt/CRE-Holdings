About Us

Our Story

CRE Holdings, LLC was founded by John Rodgers. John initially began his career as a commercial real estate broker and established a well-recognized and successful boutique commercial real estate brokerage firm in Colorado Springs, Peak Commercial Properties, which he has since exited.

As a Broker, John was exposed to real estate cycles early in his career, and was able to work and represent some of the most successful investors in Southern Colorado. With these valuable experiences, John started practicing what he preached by investing in Commercial Real Estate and later establishing CRE Holdings, LLC.

CRE Holdings, LLC thrives because of their nimble team, strategic relationships, experience vendors and management philosophy.
